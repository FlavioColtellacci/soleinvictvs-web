Three Pillars
IPhilosophia

The examined life is the precondition for all else. Stoic philosophy is a combat doctrine — tested against plague, exile, empire, and time. We read the ancients not for comfort, but for architecture.

IIDisciplina

Empires are not built on inspiration. They are built on relentless, systematic discipline applied daily without exception. The legionary did not feel like training. He trained.

IIILegio

No legionary stood alone. The cohort held the line. We are a community bound not by sentiment, but by shared standards. The Legion holds you to the mark when individual will falters.
